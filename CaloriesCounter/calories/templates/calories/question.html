Welcome in Calories Counter app!

This application is divided into two parts.


Simple part


If you would like just to control what you eat and check just how many calories, first part is for you! There is as option to add meal and you can after check what you have eaten today and how much calories have your meals had.

Advanced part


If you are sportsmen or you just want to control your weight in more details, this part is for you! You have to fill a form about your current weight, height, age and gender and add your meal, and this application will show you information about details of your today's meals. You can see there how many calories, proteins, fats and carbohydrates you should eat and check how many have you eaten. You also can see the stats from last 7 days, in let you control your calories from more days than only today.
